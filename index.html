How to Play "Letter Link"

  1. Connect: One player hosts a room, copies the code, and shares it with their partner. The other player joins.
  2. Step 1 (Starting Letter): One player chooses a starting letter for the round (e.g. L).
  3. Step 2 (Ending Letter): The other player chooses a target ending letter (e.g. E).
  4. Step 3 (The Race): Both players race to type a valid English word starting with the starting letter and ending with the ending letter (e.g. starts with L, ends with E, like LOVE).
  5. Winning: The first player to submit a valid word wins the round. We automatically query an English dictionary database, and also support a Partner Approval fallback in case of slang or local jokes!

💡 Sound effects and visual feedback keep the pace fast. Swap turns and see how many points you can rack up!
